Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until translucent.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add spices and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add 2 cups water and bring to a boil.
  6. Add soaked rice, reduce heat, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Fluff the biryani gently with a fork. Garnish with cilantro.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
