Keoti Dal Recipe

Ingredients

Daal & Vegetables:

  • 6 tablespoons Arhar dal
  • 3 tablespoons Urad dal
  • 2.5 bowls of water
  • 1 tomato (Tamatar)
  • 5 green chilies (Mircha)

Oil & Spices:

  • 1 teaspoon salt (Namak)
  • A little oil
  • 2 pinches black cumin (Kalongi Zeera)
  • 2 pinches turmeric (Haldi)
  • 3 pinches coriander powder (Dhaniya)

For Tadka (Tempering):

  • Heat oil, add 1 pinch cumin (Zeera)
  • 1/2 onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Mix 1 handful (4 tablespoons) of Arhar dal and 1/2 handful of Urad dal, wash them well, and add 2.5 bowls of water and 1 tablespoon of salt in a pressure cooker.
  2. Let the dal cook slightly, then add a little oil for flavor.
  3. Chop 2-3 medium-sized green chilies and half a tomato, and add to the dal with 2 pinches of turmeric and coriander powder.
  4. Close the cooker and wait for 4 whistles.
  5. Chop 1/2 onion for tempering.
  6. Heat oil in a pan, add 1 pinch of cumin, and sauté the onion until it turns dark brown. Immediately add this tadka to the dal in the cooker.
  7. Shake the cooker a bit, then place it back on the stove. Let it whistle once more.
  8. Your Keoti Dal is now ready to serve!
