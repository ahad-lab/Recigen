Mutton Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mutton pieces (with bone)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add the mutton pieces, onion, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, turmeric powder, chili powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Cook until the mutton is tender.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the cooked mutton mixture and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add 2 cups water and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the soaked rice and stir gently.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and fluffy.
  7. Turn off heat and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  8. Fluff the biryani gently with a fork and garnish with cilantro (if using).
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
